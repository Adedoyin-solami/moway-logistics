FOR DRIVERS [CARS, TRICYCLES AND MOTORBIKES]

How much time do drivers require to accept a trip request?

Drivers do not need more than 30 seconds to accept a ride request.

Can drivers cancel trip requests?

Yes, drivers can cancel requests but this is not advisable as it could after their ratings.

How much luggage can a driver take?

A rider can bring as much luggage as possible, provided they can fit into the trunk and/or freely in the rider compartment of the car.

How long does a driver have to wait before charges apply?

Waiting time charge is activated after the driver has arrived a pickup location for 5 minutes. The wait-time charge is on a per minute basis of NGN15.

What is paid waiting?

It is the compensation drivers get for time spent waiting for riders either at a pickup location or in between an active ride. It takes 5 minutes to activate the paid waiting. What happens if a driver have a failed payment? MOWAY has three payment options - 1. wallet 2. card 3. cash. Drivers have options in the even that one fails

What type of cars does MOWAY accept?

MOWAY only accept vehicles manufactured no later than year 2001. Other general requirements include: no commercial branding; no exterior or interior damages; must have 4 doors and a minimum of 4 passenger seats (excluding the driver’s seat).

Can a driver use multiple cars under one MOWAY account?

A driver is allowed to register/tie ONLY one vehicle to an account per time. In case of a fleet, the driver can ONLY be registered under one Fleet Partner.

Can MOWAY provide drivers with cars?

Drivers can purchase a brand new or pre-owned car through MOWAY ride and own or other options. The lowest upfront payment is NGN20% of the car value. Instalment from NGN30,000 weekly and spread over 3-4 years.

What to check for before accepting a trip request on MOWAY?

- Driver’s location and the pickup location - Estimated pickup time - Name of pinned location - Location address

Does MOWAY drivers charge riders for their luggage?

- MOWAY drivers do not charge riders for their luggage.

Can MOWAY drivers travel distance?

MOWAY drivers can take long distance trips between communities, cities and even states. However, the booking and charge rates may differ.

Can I drive for MOWAY without a personal car?

Yes, drivers without their personal cars have the option to drive for MOWAY on a defined contract basis.

Does MOWAY charge for cancellation?

Neither the driver nor the rider will be charged for cancellation provided it is within the 2-5 minutes arrival and waiting time between the driver and rider.

Why MOWAY could block a driver?

There are several reasons a driver could be blocked either temporarily or permanently. These include: - Inappropriate behaviour to riders - Multiple unresolved complaints from riders - Untimely remittances - Regular cancellation of ride requests

Does traffic affect MOWAY price?

Yes, the final price of a trip may increase if there was heavy traffic over a period exceeding the calculated trip time.

How much can drivers make from MOWAY monthly?

Drivers get paid weekly but they can also request to be paid ANYTIME irrespective how many trips. They can choose to cash out anytime. The most active MOWAY drivers can make between NGN300,000 to NGN500,000 monthly. On a weekly basis, very active drivers can make up to NGN100,000 and more

When can MOWAY drivers cash out?

MOWAY drivers can request to cash out at will. Provided they have internet connection, active bank account and correct details.

How much percentage does MOWAY charge drivers?

MOWAY charges a 15% flat commission from the final price per trip order. Commission fees apply to cash, wallet and card rides including cancellation fees and are calculated automatically and deducted from the weekly earnings. Note: Commission doesn’t apply to tips, bonuses, airport or toll fees.

How does MOWAY charge per trip?

The estimated charge per trip is based on the traveling distance and the estimated time of arrival.

How long does it take to approve a car on MOWAY?

Car activation takes an average of 72 hours. This process includes online and onset verification.

What is MOWAY policy on fuel hike?

In locations where MOWAY operates, reduction in commission fee will apply in times of fuel hike to cushion the effect on drivers.

Does MOWAY observe national public holidays for drivers?

Yes, MOWAY drivers are employees like any other. So on key public holidays, MOWAY will either discount its commission or declare it a commission-free day to celebrate drivers.

How long does it take to approve a car on MOWAY?

Car activation takes an average of 72 hours. This process includes online and onset verification.

Do I need to have a tricycle or motorbike to be a delivery rider?

Yes. You need to have a tricycle or motorbike to be a delivery rider, and a functional smartphone for seamless operation when using the MOWAY app. In this instance, MOWAY will charge a 10% commission on every order you get.

How much do you charge per delivery?

We charge NGN500 per delivery within a 3-5kilometres. An additional charge of NGN100 for every extra 1kilometre.

In the event that I do not have a tricycle or motorbike. How can I become a delivery rider for MOWAY?

You need to have a functional smartphone for seamless operation when using the MOWAY app. After you meet all the necessary requirements, MOWAY will place you on a basic salary plus additional commission on each delivery made.

How do I sign up to be a delivery rider for MOWAY?

Visit our office for a physical assessment of your tricycle or motorbike. You can also call any of our support lines to schedule an appointment.

How much would I earn as a rider for MOWAY?

Our riders are paid a basic salary of NGN30,000. They also earn an additional NGN50 on every delivery they make.
